**Music-Mood**

Music-Mood is a fun web application that recommends music based on ur current mood. Instead of spending time searching, you simply choose how ur feel, and the website gives you a matching playlist.

---

**Inspiration**

The idea came from my dad. He always tells me:

**"Music is the language of the universe."**

He believes that every mood has its own music.

---

**How It Works**

- Open the website.
- Choose your current mood.
- The website changes its colors to match your mood and give u the suitable playlist with the viral songs.

---

**Features**

- different moods
- Spotify playlists for each mood
- Dynamic background colors and Dark and Light mode
- Responsive design

---

**Languages used**

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript
